Work Experience: Facilitating Partnerships

A detailed explanation of my experience facilitating business collaborations between Spicy 2(Local Restaurants) and E Force(local internet cafes), with contract examples.

Dec 16, 2022
3 minute read

During a fortunate opportunity, I was entrusted by the owner of Spicy 2, a local restaurant, to create additional revenue streams for their business. Leveraging this trust, I initiated and facilitated collaborations between Spicy 2 and two separate businesses: E Force Internet Cafe and GIGA Internet Cafe and Script Room.

Collaboration with E Force Internet Cafe

The collaboration between Spicy 2 and E Force Internet Cafe was successfully established. The partnership involved streamlining the ordering and delivery process for customers at E Force, allowing Spicy 2 to deliver meals directly to the cafe.

Key Activities:

  • Communication Coordination: Set up a WeChat group to facilitate real-time communication between the two businesses.
  • Agreement Drafting: Created a detailed contract outlining roles, responsibilities, and payment methods.
  • Payment Processing: Implemented a mobile EFTPOS system for seamless customer payments.

Outcome:

This partnership provided E Force customers with convenient meal options while increasing Spicy 2’s sales and market visibility.

Collaboration Attempt with GIGA Internet Cafe

Despite efforts to establish a similar collaboration with GIGA Internet Cafe, the partnership did not materialize due to insufficient customer traffic at GIGA. While the agreement was well-structured, the lack of consistent demand rendered the collaboration unviable.

Key Responsibilities and Skills Demonstrated

  1. Negotiation and Mediation:

    • Facilitated discussions between parties to ensure mutual satisfaction with the collaboration terms.

  2. Contract Drafting:

    • Produced bilingual contracts that clearly defined the terms of the partnership, including delivery logistics and revenue sharing.

  3. Problem-Solving:

    • Addressed logistical challenges such as delivery timelines and payment processing to streamline operations.

  4. Market Assessment:

    • Evaluated the feasibility of partnerships based on customer demand and business viability.

Outcome and Learning

The collaboration with E Force Internet Cafe successfully increased Spicy 2’s revenue and expanded its customer base. Although the partnership with GIGA Internet Cafe did not succeed, the experience taught me the importance of assessing market conditions and demand before pursuing business relationships.

This experience enhanced my skills in business negotiation, communication, and problem-solving while demonstrating my ability to create mutually beneficial partnerships that drive growth and profitability.
